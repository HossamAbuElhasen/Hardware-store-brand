GDA Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GDA POS, Barcode, Cash Handling & Security Solutions

Are GDA receipt printers compatible with all POS systems?

Yes. GDA receipt printers support ESC/POS commands and are compatible with most POS systems. They offer USB, LAN and optional Bluetooth connectivity.

Do GDA barcode printers support thermal and thermal transfer printing?

Yes. Our barcode printers support both direct thermal and thermal transfer technology, making them ideal for retail, warehouses and industrial labeling.

Are GDA scanners available in wired and wireless models?

Absolutely. GDA provides high-speed 1D and 2D barcode scanners in both wired and wireless versions for retail and warehouse environments.

Do GDA money counting machines detect counterfeit notes?

Yes. GDA money counters are equipped with UV, MG and IR counterfeit detection systems to ensure maximum accuracy and security during cash handling.

What types of safes does GDA offer?

GDA offers office safes, full armored fireproof safes, semi-armored safes and hotel safes with advanced digital locking systems.

Do you provide warranty and technical support?

Yes. All GDA products come with official warranty and professional technical support to ensure long-term reliability and performance.

Can I request bulk pricing for commercial projects?

Yes. We provide competitive wholesale pricing and customized solutions for supermarkets, warehouses, hotels and large commercial businesses.
